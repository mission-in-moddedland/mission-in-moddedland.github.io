Submission Guidelines

When you submit a mod to our database, you agree to the Submission Guidelines, as stated below.

1. Keep your content PG-13 or below.

Content on our site can be put into the following rating groups: G, PG, and PG-13. If your mod doesn't fit these rating groups, then it is not eligible to be added to our database.

2. Keep your content malware free.

When a mod is checked, before we even play it, we check the source code. Due to this, any mods that are obfuscated and/or encrypted will not be added to the database. We also run a VirusTotal scan, to ensure that no malware is found in the mod's files.

3. No leaking.

Mission in Moddedland is a leak-free website. You are not allowed to leak content in your mod. This includes enabling features that are being worked on at the time of the release, etc.

4. Have common sense.

If there's something not listed here, but you have a feeling that it'll probably break our rules, it probably will. Our guidelines can't cover everything, but if it makes sense not to submit it, then don't.
